Equations

  1. Elastic trial state:

    1.1 \(\sigma = E * (\varepsilon - \varepsilon_P)\)

    1.2 \(E_t = E\)

  2. Yield check: \(f = ||\sigma|| - f_y\)

  3. IF \(f \ge 0\):

    3.1. \(\Delta\varepsilon_P = \text{sign}(\sigma) * \frac{f}{E}\)

    3.2. \(\sigma = \sigma - E * \Delta\varepsilon_P\)

    3.3. \(E_t = E_t - E\)
